How Mobile Mining Works

Unlike traditional cryptocurrencies that require specialized, energy-intensive hardware, PhiCoin uses a unique consensus mechanism specifically designed for mobile devices. This approach not only makes cryptocurrency mining accessible to everyone but also drastically reduces the environmental impact.

Our algorithm intelligently manages device resources, ensuring mining only happens when your phone is idle, plugged in, or has sufficient battery. The process is optimized to minimize battery usage and heat generation while maximizing mining efficiency.

The network verifies transactions through a collaborative proof-of-participation system where many devices each do a small amount of work, rather than a few powerful miners doing all the heavy lifting.

Getting Started With Mobile Mining

Coming Soon

Once launched, starting your PhiCoin mining journey will be simple:

  1. Download the PhiCoin wallet app from your device's app store
  2. Create a new wallet or import an existing one
  3. Navigate to the Mining section and toggle mining on
  4. Customize your mining preferences (when to mine, resource usage limits, etc.)
  5. Start earning PhiCoin while contributing to the network!

The app will include built-in optimization features that automatically adjust mining intensity based on your device's current state, ensuring you earn PhiCoin without impacting your phone's performance or battery life.
