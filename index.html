Tokenomics in the Golden Ratio

The economic foundations of PhiCoin are inspired by the harmonious proportions of the Golden Ratio.

1.618 Billion Maximum Supply

The maximum supply of PhiCoin is set at 1.618 billion - inspired by the Golden Ratio number (1.618), creating natural scarcity while allowing for widespread adoption.


Balanced Distribution

38.2% of coins will be initially distributed, while 61.8% will be released through mining/staking over time - another Golden Ratio proportion for perfect balance.


Sustainable Economics

An innovative fee model with burning mechanism ensures a self-regulating currency that is neither inflationary nor extremely deflationary - maintaining stability for long-term growth.
